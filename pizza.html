This will be the best pizza you have ever made before!

Ingredients

  • Dough Ball
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Bake crust for 10 min at 350
  2. Add pizza sauce
  3. Add mozzerella on top
  4. Add seasoning on top of that
  5. Bake for 10 more minutes
